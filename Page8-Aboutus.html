ABOUT LIBRAUX

LIBRAUX is a digital library management system designed to streamline the process of monitoring, borrowing, and returning academic resources. The system features a centralized catalog and an automated tracking module to ensure efficient management of book due dates and inventory status. This project focuses on providing a functional yet aesthetically minimal interface tailored for IT students and faculty.
